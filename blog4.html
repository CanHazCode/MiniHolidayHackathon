Blog 4: My Top 5 Things I love

5. Anything is something to love if you are willing to find joy in it

There was boring, colourful paper on the art table and I just with my imagination I turned it into confetti! That's for parties. Mommy is gonna love the surprise!

4. Toys

I like things that move fast. Once I had a bat toy that I caught in the attic, but when I brought it to the bedroom to play catch, it only played a little while. Then it wouldn't play again :(

3. Food!

I love tasting new treats. Mommy and I eat together everyday and it's the best. She always gives me all the meats I want and I don't have to eat my vegetables.

2. My Baggie

I get very mad when anyone takes my baggie. It is mine. You cannot haz!

1. Mommy

I tell her everyday. Plus I take the best care of her. I know cos she tells me!
