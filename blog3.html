Blog 3: My Top 5 Places to Haz a Cat Nap

5. Outside

Sunshine and Shade are good for kittens. Mommy doesn't touch grass. So I make sure to go outside and roll in the dirt till I collect enough to put some in her bed so she can sleep well and be healthy too

4. By the bookshelf with the Catnip

There is a door there and I face the breeze and roll around till I fall asleep and dream about sleeping in catnip

3. In a hiding spot

I love it when I fast asleep peacefully and mommy is stressed looking for me and can't see me in my nooks. No awareness *shakes cute wittle head*

2. With Hoomans

Soft kitties are built for cuddles so I never miss a chance to steal some heat. Remember to sleep by their feets if you don't want pets. Sleep on their chest if you want mega cuddles <3

1. Anywhere my Mommy is

It is comforting to know my mommy is right near me. She will protect me and pet me and feed me and love. Hmm.. Where is mommy?
